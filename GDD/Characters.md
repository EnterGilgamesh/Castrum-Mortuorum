## Castrum Mortuorum

The Castle of the Dead
### Brief Description:

A tell-tale sign of a broken mind.
Was once the happy place of the brain, now turned against it.
#### Main Roles:

The main setting
==Potentially it's own character?==
##### Appearance:

Was once a dream-like fort. A place with many rooms, each a comforting place from memory.

Now a husk of it's former self. Covered in veins growing like vines, bricks of flesh and blood, tower rooves made of bone and a menacing clocktower atop the final story.
Grimly sat in a grotesque hellish wasteland.
